Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I book a train ticket?

Answer: You can book a train ticket through our website by navigating to the "Book Ticket" section and following the step-by-step instructions.

2. What information do I need to provide for booking a ticket?

Answer: To book a ticket, you'll need to provide your name, departure city, destination city, travel date, and the number of seats you want to book.
